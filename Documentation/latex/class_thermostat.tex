\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a thermostat device that can adjust the temperature of a room. 

The \doxylink{class_thermostat}{Thermostat} class inherits from \doxylink{class_settable_device}{Settable\+Device} and provides functionalities to heat a room, display its status, and retrieve its energy consumption and thread level.
